Impact :

The COVID-19 infections and deaths have largely been uneven within and between countries.
With 17% of the world’s population, India has so far had 13% of global COVID-19 infections and 8.5% of deaths.
Maharashtra accounting for 9% of India’s population, is the worst affected state, with 19% of infections and 33% of total deaths in the country until 23rd December 2020.
